What is Glitch Text?

Glitch text, also known as corrupted text or zalgo text, is a form of text art that uses Unicode combining characters to create distorted, creepy, or visually striking effects. It originated from internet culture and has become popular in gaming communities, social media, and digital art.

How It Works

Our text generator uses Unicode character combinations and special algorithms to create various glitch effects. For the Zalgo effect, it combines multiple diacritic marks with each character. The Craziness Level slider controls how many marks are added to each character, allowing you to create subtle to extreme effects. All processing happens in your browser - no data is sent to our servers.
